Enhance Your Golf Experience
There is no better way to experience your round of golf at Hole in One than taking a trained forecaddie. Hole in One has partnered with CADDIEMASTER to enhance the overall golf experience with offering forecaddies.

Aside from having extensive course knowledge to help you navigate around the Lisbon desert golf experience, our forecaddies will assist in locating balls in the desert, raking bunkers, cleaning your clubs and just creating a much more memorable experience.
