About Us

Welcome to Hole in One, a premier golf resort located on a beautiful stretch of coastline.

Since opening in 2005, we have provided a first-class golfing experience for players of all skill levels.

Our championship courses offer stunning views and are sculpted into the natural terrain for a challenging, yet enjoyable round.

We are dedicated to ensuring the comfort and enjoyment of our guests on and off the course.
